Photography Tips

Light is the paint of photography. Without this fundamental element, no photo could be taken.

  1. Don’t squeeze the photographer to photoshoot your property if it is not a sunny day.
  2. Always keep curtains open for the maximum natural light source.
  3. You property should be neat and clean, and hide any piece of furniture that could be a distractive object.
  4. Turn on your lights, they work perfectly with natural light.
